## в—Ҳ WHAT IS THIS

**Sovereign Command Simulator** is a **real-time geopolitical war game** that puts you in control of an entire nation вҖ” its economy, military, diplomacy, intelligence apparatus, and population вҖ” simultaneously.

This isn't Civilization. This isn't Risk. This is what happens when **Palantir Gotham**, **a NORAD war room**, and **a sovereign central bank** collide inside a browser.

You control **everything**:
- Fire ICBMs and watch them arc across a real D3-geo world map in real time
- Crash foreign economies through sanctions, currency manipulation, and bond market attacks
- Rig elections, fund proxy militias, and deploy HAARP weather weapons
- Watch your cities glow with nuclear exclusion zones that **never heal**
- Manage a living world of 50+ nations and sub-state actors вҖ” all running their own autonomous AI agendas

**The world runs whether you act or not. History waits for no one.**

## в—Ҳ FEATURES

### рҹ—әпёҸ Live Intelligence Theater Map
- **Real D3-geo TopoJSON projection** вҖ” actual world geography, not placeholder polygons
- **Toggleable intelligence layers**: Political / Military / Economic / Cyber / Weather / Propaganda / Population
- **Live missile arc animations** вҖ” Bezier-curved strike paths with animated dash-march in real time
- **Conflict pulse rings** вҖ” pulsing red rings at every active war zone
- **Consequence scars** вҖ” nuclear exclusion zones, rubble fields, and HAARP flood zones persist permanently on the map
- **Fog of War** вҖ” countries you haven't invested intelligence in show as `[CLASSIFIED]`
- **Globe mode** вҖ” toggle between flat map and 3D orthographic rotating globe with drag-to-rotate

### рҹҺ–пёҸ Three Sovereign Roles (Switch Anytime)
| Role | What You Control |
|------|-----------------|

| **INTELLIGENCE ANALYST** | Covert ops, CIA dossiers, propaganda campaigns, HUMINT networks, cyber intrusion |

- **14 weapon types**: ICBM, SLBM, Hypersonic, Cruise Missile, Fighter Jet, Stealth Bomber, Carrier Group, Submarine, Tank Division, Artillery, Drone Swarm, Cyber Weapon, EMP Device, Dirty Bomb
- **Fuel logistics**: Every unit tracks fuel levels. Ground your own fleet by running out of oil
- **Pilot morale**: Low approval rating + ongoing war = your pilots fly at 60% effectiveness
- **Supply chain degradation**: Sanctions sever your resupply lines in real time
- **ABM intercept system**: Iron Dome V1/V2/V3 upgrades, hypersonic missiles that evade ALL V1/V2 shields
- **Nuclear authorization**: Double-confirm launch with a 10-second countdown and authorization code entry
- **Permanent nuclear fallout**: Nuked zones stay on the map. Forever.

### рҹ’° God-Mode Economics
- **Full sovereign finance**: GDP, inflation, debt-to-GDP, currency strength, bond markets, interest rates
- **Printing press**: Activate it. Watch inflation spike. Watch your currency collapse. Watch civil unrest explode.
- **Oligarch networks**: Bribe them, exile them, or nationalize their assets вҖ” each choice has cascading consequences
- **Black market arms bazaar**: Bid against AI nations in real-time arms auctions at 40-80% premium
- **Commodity spot exchange**: Oil, rare earth, silicon, wheat, weapons-grade uranium вҖ” all with live price history charts
- **Offshore slush funds**: Move money through black budget channels undetected

### рҹ•өпёҸ Intelligence Operations
- **Covert ops**: Assassinate leaders, rig elections, hack power grids, fund rebel factions, deploy ransomware

- **50+ sovereign nations** + **12 sub-state actors** (ISIS, Hezbollah, Wagner Group, Houthis, Hamas, PKK, and more)

- **Internal factions** within each country: Military Hardliners, Reformers, Oligarchs, Islamists, Separatists вҖ” each with their own demands and rebellion thresholds
- **Pre-war tension phases**: No nation jumps straight to war. Watch relations deteriorate through HOSTILE вҶ’ CRISIS TALKS вҶ’ AT WAR

- **Live commodity ticker**: Scrolling prices for oil, rare earth, silicon, uranium, and arms always visible

## в—Ҳ HOW TO PLAY

### Getting Started

```
1. Open https://geopolitics-war-sim.pages.dev/
2. Watch the WebGL globe intro (or click to skip)
3. Choose a scenario or SANDBOX mode
4. Select your nation from 50+ sovereign states
5. Configure mission parameters:
   - Duration: SCENARIO / TIMED (N ticks) / ENDLESS
   - Tick scale: 1 DAY / 1 WEEK / 1 MONTH per tick
   - AI aggression, nuclear doctrine, fog of war settings
6. Click [INITIATE MISSION]
7. The world is now yours. Break it wisely.
```

## в—Ҳ STRATEGIES

### рҹҹў The Economic Strangler
*Win without firing a single missile*

1. Max out your intelligence budget and CYBER_FIREWALL research early
2. Build a SIGINT network covering all major rivals
3. Target their bond markets: use `/op FINANCIAL_ATTACK` when their debt stress > 60%
4. Deploy disinformation campaigns to spike their domestic unrest
5. Offer trade deals and foreign aid to their neighbors, peeling off their allies
6. When their treasury hits $0 and coup risk hits 90%, do nothing. Let their own military remove them.

**Countered by**: Nations with printing press active (infinite cash) or QUANTUM_COMMS research (immune to propaganda)

---

### рҹ”ҙ The Preemptive Doctrine
*Strike before the window closes*

1. Immediately invest all R&D into `HYPERSONIC_TECH` (bypasses all ABM V1/V2 shields)
2. Build a `KILL_SAT` in LEO and destroy enemy EARLY_WARNING satellites вҖ” they won't see the launch coming
3. Use `/op HACK_GRID` on the target to disable their radar networks 3 ticks before launch
4. Queue a full hypersonic salvo targeting military assets, not cities (keeps world opinion recoverable)
5. Follow up with `/op FINANCIAL_ATTACK` on their treasury the tick after impact
6. Immediately offer ceasefire at maximum diplomatic leverage

**Risk**: If EARLY_WARNING is active you get 3 ticks of retaliation. If they have `IRON_DOME_V3` + `EMP_SHIELD`, your hypersonics still get intercepted.

---

### рҹ”ө The Shadow Patron
*Control the world through proxies*

1. Choose a great power (US or CN) for maximum black budget starting capacity
2. Identify 3-4 sub-state actors in regions you want to destabilize: `/actor fund HEZBOLLAH 2`, `/actor arm HOUTHIS`, etc.
3. Keep your own nation officially neutral вҖ” never declare war directly
4. Active actors against your rivals' allies: they absorb the military cost, you absorb none
5. Meanwhile: build trade relationships with everyone else, issuing bonds to fund their development
6. After 40 ticks, your proxies have fought your wars, your rivals are exhausted, and you have 8+ trade partners
7. Dominate the global economy while everyone else rebuilds

**Risk**: If any covert op blows back (traceability > 60%), your proxies get exposed and you face UN resolutions and opinion crashes globally.

---

### рҹҹ  The Currency Bomber
*Make a country's money worthless without touching its military*

1. Wait until target nation activates their printing press (common in AI nations under economic stress)
2. Short their commodities on the spot exchange вҖ” especially OIL and RARE_EARTH if they're a producer
3. Issue a tariff at 30% on all their exports to destabilize their trade surplus
4. Lobby a UN resolution imposing financial sanctions (get 3 votes lined up first)
5. The combination: printing press + tariffs + sanctions + commodity shorts = inflation > 15% within 20 ticks
6. At 15% inflation: their `currencyStrength` collapses below 60, unrest spikes, factions destabilize
7. Their military budget gets auto-cut as debt service consumes revenue. They can no longer maintain their arsenal readiness.

**Countered by**: Nations that have `QUANTUM_COMMS` (immune to sanction coordination) or a friendly P5 nation blocking your UN resolutions.

---

### рҹҹЈ The Long Game
*Build an empire that cannot be threatened*

1. Start SANDBOX mode. Set tension to `COLD PEACE`. Choose a mid-tier nation (AU, TR, or BR).
2. Spend the first 30 ticks doing nothing but: `IRON_DOME_V3` вҶ’ `CYBER_FIREWALL_V3` вҶ’ `SATELLITE_RECON` вҶ’ `QUANTUM_COMMS`
3. While researching, issue bonds to 3-4 neighbors вҖ” you become their creditor. They won't attack their bank.
4. Maintain 8+ trade partners. Every partner adds GDP growth and locks them into your orbit diplomatically.
5. By tick 50: you have impenetrable defenses, the best intel, economic leverage over neighbors, and no enemies.
6. Now you can afford to play offense anywhere in the world вҖ” from a position that cannot be threatened.

**Weakness**: Pure defense is slow. If a nuclear power targets you at DEFCON 1 before your shields are complete, you're exposed for 20-30 ticks.

---

### вҳўпёҸ The Nuclear Shadow
*Make peace through irradiation*

> "We have enough nuclear weapons to destroy the world several times over. This is exactly as many as we need."

1. Immediately build SLBM capacity (submarine-launched = 20% harder to intercept than ICBMs)
2. Research `IRON_DOME_V3` (your own ABM) and `ORBITAL_WARFARE_V1` (orbital kill satellites)
3. Destroy all enemy EARLY_WARNING satellites вҖ” they lose 3-tick advance notice of your launches
4. Never fire. Just move your submarines. Make sure everyone's intelligence knows where they are.
5. Any nation that approaches hostile opinion will calculate the cost and back down
6. Use the permanent threat to extract diplomatic concessions, trade deals, and bond purchases

**Risk**: If someone deploys `IRON_DOME_V3` AND `EMP_SHIELD` AND keeps EARLY_WARNING satellites operational, your nuclear deterrent is weakened. They may call the bluff.

## в—Ҳ DUAL CLOCK SYSTEM

Two clocks run simultaneously at all times:

```
рҹ“…  14 MAR 2027          вҖ” In-game calendar date (advances per tick)
вҸұ   01:23:45             вҖ” Real session time (wall clock, always running)
```

You choose your tick scale at game start:
- **1 TICK = 1 DAY** вҶ’ Full conflict cycles unfold over real game-weeks
- **1 TICK = 1 WEEK** вҶ’ Balanced вҖ” recommended for most scenarios
- **1 TICK = 1 MONTH** вҶ’ Strategic вҖ” decades pass in a single session

Game duration:
- **SCENARIO** вҶ’ Ends on win/loss condition only
- **TIMED** вҶ’ You set the tick budget. Scored at the end.
- **ENDLESS** вҶ’ No end state. The world burns or thrives as long as you play.
